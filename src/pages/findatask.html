Find a task

On the Conversation tab, type “@eTask” and choose eTask, and then click on “Find a Task”.

Use a keyword to find a task .

Task will be embedded onto Conversation tab.

  • Open Task: Microsoft Teams will be directed to that task in eTask tab.
  • Attached message (Optional): Write a comment or tag someone to improve concentration.
  • Send: Send the whole message onto Conversation tab.
