My Project

Allow user to have a look at all projects that being managed by user.

Here is default screen of “My Projects”:

By default, grid is grouped by projects. Users can see all projects being managed by them.

  • All functions in “My Projects” are as same as in “My Tasks” excluded “Sync to Outlook”.
  • Tasks in “My Projects” are from projects that user is the manager.

Note: Users won't see the tab My Projects when they are not in the management team of any project.
